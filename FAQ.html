Frequently Asked Questions

Computer system services involve tasks related to setting up, configuring, and maintaining computer systems and networks. This can include hardware installation, software configuration, network setup, and ensuring systems run efficiently and securely.

Proper configuration ensures that systems run efficiently and securely. It helps avoid conflicts, ensures software and hardware work together properly, and contributes to better performance and stability.

Cable installation and routing refer to the process of setting up physical cables for networking and power in a computer system or network. Proper routing ensures efficient data flow, reduces the risk of interference, and promotes a tidy and organized setup.

It depends on your expertise and the complexity of the setup. For simple home networks, you can usually manage basic cable installation. However, for complex setups or corporate environments, it's best to hire professionals to ensure proper installation, routing, and compliance with safety regulations.

Drivers are software components that enable the operating system to communicate with hardware devices like printers, graphics cards, and sound cards. Without proper drivers, hardware might not function correctly or at all.
